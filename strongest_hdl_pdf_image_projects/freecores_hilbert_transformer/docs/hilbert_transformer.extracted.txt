1 Introduction
The Hilbert Transform is an important component in communication systems, e.g.
for single sideband modulation/demodulation, amplitude and phase detection, etc. It
can be formulated as ﬁltering operation which makes it possible to approximate the
Hilbert Transform with a digital ﬁlter. Due to the non-causal and inﬁnite impulse
response of that ﬁlter, it is not that easy to get a good approximation with low
hardware resource usage. Therefore, diﬀerent ﬁlters with diﬀerent complexities have
been implemented. The detailed discussion can be found in [KS08].
